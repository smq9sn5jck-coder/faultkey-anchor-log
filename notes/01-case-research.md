# FaultKey Anchor Log — Stage A: Case Research

**Purpose:** Identify 8–10 active AI-liability cases (filed 2024–2026, NOT yet resolved as of 22 May 2026 AEST) suitable for sealed-prediction methodology.

**Selection criteria:**
1. **Active** — filed but not finally resolved (no settlement publicly disclosed, no final judgment)
2. **Public facts** — enough public information to construct a credible FaultKey scenario input from court filings, regulator statements, or journalism
3. **Mix of jurisdictions** — AU, US, UK, EU (priority to AU given FaultKey's AU-first positioning)
4. **Mix of fault patterns** — vendor at fault, deployer at fault, user at fault, shared
5. **Mix of sectors** — finance, healthcare, hiring, government, content moderation, autonomous systems
6. **Reasonable resolution horizon** — likely to resolve within 6–24 months (anchor log payoff window)
7. **Public visibility on resolution** — media-watched cases so resolution is verifiable

**Excluded:**
- Settled cases (no blind-prediction value)
- Cases sealed under confidentiality
- Cases where AI involvement is alleged but not yet evidenced
